Sports can be categorized into various types based on different criteria, including the nature of the activity, the environment in which it is played, and the primary objective of the sport . Sports can be enjoyed both competitively and recreationally, and they play a significant role in promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and entertainment worldwide. Team sports are a category of athletic activities where two or more teams compete against each other with the goal of achieving victory. These sports often emphasize teamwork, cooperation, and strategy among team members. Team sports are not only a source of entertainment but also promote physical fitness, social interaction, and personal growth. They can be played at various levels, from casual recreational leagues to highly competitive professional sports.

Individual sports are activities in which athletes compete on their own without the need for a team. These sports often emphasize personal skill, physical fitness, and individual performance. In individual sports, athletes are responsible for their own success. They must rely on their personal skills, training, and determination to achieve their goals. There are no teammates to assist or rely upon during competition. Individual sports often require rigorous and focused training.

Combat sports are a category of athletic activities that involve direct physical contact between competitors, with the primary objective being to defeat or subdue the opponent within a set of rules and regulations. These sports often require a combination of physical conditioning, technique, strategy, and mental toughness. Combat sports involve physical contact between competitors, often in the form of striking, grappling, or both. Each combat sport has its own specific rules and regulations
